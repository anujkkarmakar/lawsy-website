What makes us different?

Quizes

Interactive audio-visual questions focused on laws applicable in daily life; presented to you on demand and through daily notifications. Performance in the quizzes decides your rating, i.e. expertise level. The higher the rating, the more difficult questions you will get next. Area(city/state/country) based leaderboards will be displayed to motivate in learning more.

Courses and Articles

Interactive courses serving you knowledge on various types of law, i.e. civil, criminal etc. Completion of the courses gives you more rating points. Artificial intelligence (AI) based recommendation system that will suggest specific courses focused on your areas of weakness in understanding. Besides we will provide daily law related particular age specific articles to keep our kiddos updated.

Forums

Post your questions and engage in discussions with your peers, or seek expert advice on your questions by requesting expert help. Additional option to create a query report from your question and send to concerned law and order government body or to NGOs registered on our platform. Large Language Model (LLM/Generative AI) based automatic answer bot which will suggest answers to your questions immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be quizes of different levels?

Quiz will be of 3 different levels. The higher level quiz only gets unlocked once the lower level is successfully accomplished.

Is there any revenue stream associated with it?

At large scale, revenue streams to explore will be a. Subscription fee from law firms (or individual lawyers) to use our platform to answer forum questions, as that will help them build credibility among the youth and gain future clients. b. Selling learning modules to corporations for training of their employees on various corporate/business laws.

Is internet required to access Lawsy?

The backend of Lawsy uses Amazon AWS. Internet connectivity issues can impact the functionality.

Can students track their progress?

Students can track their progress, giving an insight about the depth of their knowledge. There will be an area-based leaderboard in the app where they can progress of their peers.
